Emotional Detection
Project

Facial Expression Detection.

Embarking on the journey of exploring the intricacies of human emotions, I embarked on a project aimed at developing an emotion detection system. Leveraging the power of machine learning (ML) algorithms and the versatility of the Haar cascade algorithm, I endeavored to create a solution capable of discerning and categorizing various emotional states depicted in facial expressions. This project, though relatively straightforward in its approach, presented an exciting opportunity to delve into the realms of computer vision and artificial intelligence. Through diligent research, experimentation, and iteration, I sought to construct a robust and efficient system capable of understanding and interpreting the complexities of human emotion. The goal is to build a machine learning model that can accurately classify emotions such as happiness, sadness, anger, fear, and surprise based on the visual cues of facial expressions.
